Sverre Stausland

I am a Professor of General Linguistics and Scandinavian Linguistics in the Department of Linguistics and Scandinavian Studies at the University of Oslo.
I work on language change within the fields of phonology, historical linguistics, and sociolinguistics.

E-mail: sverre dot stausland at gmail dot com
